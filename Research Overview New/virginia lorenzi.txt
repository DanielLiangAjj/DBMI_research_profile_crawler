Researcher: virginia lorenzi
Research Overview:
Virginia Lorenzi's research primarily focuses on the intersection of communication and medical informatics, emphasizing the importance of controlled vocabularies in the context of hospital administration and interinstitutional relations. Through the evaluation of educational programs in graduate settings and the roles of laboratories, her work seeks to enhance the effectiveness of information systems within healthcare settings, particularly in New York and New York City. By examining these relationships and frameworks, Lorenzi aims to contribute to the improvement of medical education and the overall efficiency of health services through better communication and resource management.
