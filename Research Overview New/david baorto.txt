Researcher: david baorto
Research Overview:
David Baorto's research primarily focuses on the intersection of medical informatics and hospital administration, with a particular emphasis on the integration and management of hospital information systems. His work highlights the importance of standardized terminologies and communication in enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of computerized medical record systems within university hospitals. By exploring topics such as systems integration, interinstitutional relations, and the development of controlled vocabularies, Baorto aims to improve hospital management practices and facilitate better healthcare delivery, particularly within the urban context of New York City. His contributions to the field underscore the critical role of technology and standardized communication in modern healthcare environments.
