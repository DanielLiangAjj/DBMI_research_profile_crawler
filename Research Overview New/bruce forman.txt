Researcher: bruce forman
Research Overview:
Bruce Forman's research primarily focuses on the intersection of education and informatics within the medical field, particularly as it pertains to graduate education in New York City. His work emphasizes the evaluation of educational programs related to information systems in medical informatics, which highlights the importance of assessing the effectiveness of curricula offered by universities. Through his research, Forman aims to enhance the training and competencies of graduate students in this rapidly evolving discipline, ultimately contributing to the advancement of healthcare through improved information management and technology integration.
