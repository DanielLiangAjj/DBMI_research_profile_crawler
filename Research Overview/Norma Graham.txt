Researcher: Norma Graham
Research Overview:
Norma Graham's research primarily focuses on the intricate relationship between visual perception and physiological adaptation mechanisms in humans. Her work delves into key areas such as contrast sensitivity and how it affects pattern recognition, highlighting the complexities of sensory thresholds and spatial perception. By examining these aspects, Graham contributes to a deeper understanding of how individuals adapt physiologically to varying visual stimuli, ultimately enhancing our comprehension of visual processing and perception in human beings.
