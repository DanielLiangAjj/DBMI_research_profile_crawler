Researcher: Olena Mamykina
Research Overview:
Olena Mamykina's research predominantly revolves around the interplay of citizen science and telemedicine, particularly in the context of aging populations affected by the COVID-19 pandemic. By leveraging citizen involvement in health-related data collection and analysis, her work explores innovative approaches to enhance healthcare delivery and accessibility for older adults. The integration of telemedicine into her studies suggests a focus on utilizing technological advancements to support aging individuals during health crises. Ultimately, Mamykina's research contributes to a deeper understanding of how participatory methods and digital health solutions can improve health outcomes for seniors, especially in unprecedented times such as a global pandemic.
